Project 691 is a FIRST Robotics team composed of students from all over the William S. Hart Union High School District. Our team, originally founded in 2000, has expanded throughout the years to become the largest robotics team in the Santa Clarita Valley, with high school members (known to us as “agents”) from Academy of the Canyons Middle College High School (AOC), Hart High School (Hart), Valencia High School (VHS), and West Ranch High School (WRHS). We are based at AOC, but build at WRHS.

As proud agents of Project 691, our mission is to promote education in science and technology through experiences gained on an exciting student-led, mentor-guided high school robotics team. Our program emphasizes leadership, self-confidence, Gracious Professionalism®, Coopertition® (cooperative competition), and team work, as well as the value of individual diversity, combined creativity, and positive achievements in both our organization and our community.
